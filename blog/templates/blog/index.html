Do you want to explore the hills of Kildare, Wicklow and Dublin? Do you want to walk in the wilds and take in Nature at its very best. Do you want to meet people and have a chat in beautiful surroundings. This is the Group for you.

We walk every Wednesday, meeting at Boreen , Lusk, Sallins Rd Naas. Usually at 9.00 to share cars etc.

You will need Hill Walking gear. See the list on the menu above.

If you are interested why not contact Ann at ann@ann.ie to be included on the Mailing List. We would love to see you.

Don't worry about the pace. We are not interested in breaking world records. So come and join us.
